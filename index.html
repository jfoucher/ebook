An eBook reader for Firefox OS

This app handles epub books. When you start the app the first time, it finds all the epub files from your sdcard and imports them. This process can take a few minutes depending on the number of books and the size of the books.

When the processing is complete you can read any book by clicking on it in the list. When you are reading a book, you can use a pinch or spread finger gesture to change the font size. The small arrows at bottom right let you go to the next or previous chapter.
